Come see what all the hype is about!

Easter Island Bed & Breakfast is nestled on a secluded island in the middle of the Pacific Ocean. Our property boasts the privacy and beauty of a secluded island, yet we are only 8 hours flight from your nearest coastal city! At Easter Island Bed & Breakfast, we strive to offer our guests the highest quality Easter Island lodging. You will find our beach front cabins provide a peaceful retreat located conveniently near all of Easter Island's many attractions. When you stay with us, you will find that we offer more than just lodging. From our beautiful grounds and cozy cabin suites, to our complimentary snack cabin, which is open 24 hours a day and loaded with a variety of goodies to tempt you pallet. We want you to feel as if our home is your home, while you are here. Making your vacation unforgettable, is our ultimate goal here at Easter Island Bed & Breakfast. So, sneak away from the hustle and bustle of everyday life for a bit, and let us pamper you! A vacation filled with fun and relaxation awaits at Easter Island Bed & Breakfast.
