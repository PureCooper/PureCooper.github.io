Biography

Cooper Brown is a sophomore computer science major attending Ozarks Technical Community College with asperations in cyber security, machine learning, web development, and video game development. Prior to studying at OTC, Cooper attended Missouri University of Science & Technology where he was apart of the student design 'Mars Rover' team working on machine learning code meant to recognize QR codes. Cooper has taken a multitude of classes ranging from intermediate C# to novice web development. As a result, he is proficient in C#, Python, HTML/CSS, as well as version control using GIT and TFS. Cooper is anticipated to transfer to Missouri State University in the fall of 2023 where he will study computer science, computer information science, and cyber security.
